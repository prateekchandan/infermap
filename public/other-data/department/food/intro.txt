<p><em style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit; background-color: initial;">Production engineering/Food engineering' is a multidisciplinary field of applied <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physical_sciences">physical sciences</a> which combines science, microbiology, and engineering education for food and related industries. Food engineering includes, but is not limited to, the application of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agricultural_engineering">agricultural engineering</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mechanical_engineering">mechanical engineering</a> and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chemical_engineering">chemical engineering</a> principles to food materials. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_engineers">Food engineers</a> provide the technological knowledge transfer essential to the cost-effective production and commercialization of food products and services.</em></p><p>Food engineering is a very wide field of activities. Prospective major employers for food engineers include companies involved in food processing, food machinery, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Packaging">packaging</a>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ingredient">ingredient</a> manufacturing, instrumentation, and control. Firms that design and build food processing plants, consulting firms, government agencies, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pharmaceutical">pharmaceutical</a> companies, and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Health-care">health-care</a> firms also hire food engineers. Among its domain of knowledge and action are:</p><ul>
<li>research and development of new foods, biological and pharmaceutical products</li><li>development and operation of manufacturing, packaging and distributing systems for drug/food products</li><li>design and installation of food/biological/pharmaceutical production processes</li><li>design and operation of environmentally responsible waste treatment systems</li><li>marketing and technical support for manufacturing plants.</li></ul>
