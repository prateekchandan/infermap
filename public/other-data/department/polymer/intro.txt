<p><b style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; text-align: inherit; background-color: initial;">Polymer engineering</b> is generally an <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Engineering" style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit;">engineering</a> field that designs, analyses, and/or modifies polymer materials. Polymer engineering covers aspects of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petrochemical_industry" style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit;">petrochemical industry</a>,<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polymerization" style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit;">polymerization</a>, structure and characterization of polymers, properties of polymers, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compounding" style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; font-weight: inherit; text-align: inherit;">compounding</a> and processing of polymers and description of major polymers, structure property relations and applications.</p>
