<p><strong>ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA</strong></p><ul>
<li>Candidates must have completed their 10+2 from a recognized Board in the country</li><li>Candidates who will be appearing for their Class XII or equivalent in 2015 are also eligible to apply for BITSAT 2015.</li><li>Candidates must have secured a <strong>minimum of 75% </strong>in aggregate in the subjects of <strong>Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry(PCM)</strong></li><li>In addition, they must have secured a <strong>minimum of 60% in each subject</strong> separately.</li><li>Candidates must be proficient in English.</li><li>Candidates who have passed their 10+2 in  2012 or before are not eligible.</li></ul>
