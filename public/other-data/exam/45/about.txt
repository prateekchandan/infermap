<p><strong style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; text-align: inherit; background-color: initial;">Karnataka CET 2014</strong> is the common entrance test which is organized by the Karnataka Examination Authority to get admission in the engineering courses at graduate level in the state of Karnataka. Candidates must meet the eligibility criteria of <strong style="font-family: inherit; font-size: inherit; text-align: inherit; background-color: initial;">KCET 2014</strong> before applying for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test 2014.</p>
