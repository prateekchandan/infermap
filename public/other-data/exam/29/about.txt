<p>GUJCET 2016 - The GUJCET 2016 admissions will be organized to enroll the students in the undergraduate engineering courses by the GSEB i.e. the official authority for the admission procedure. It is obligatory to appear in JEE main examination as the admissions will be granted as according to the JEE Main scores. The GUJCET Exam has been scrapped. The aspirant must note that there is no separate exam will be held for the same. Those candidates who would like to apply for this procedure must check their eligibility criteria first and then proceed towards the filling of the application form which is expected to be released after the declaration of result of JEE main i.e. June 2016. The candidates can check out all the relevant information related to GUJCET here..</p>
